Alapalli Village

Alapalli is a Village in Aheri Taluka in Gadchiroli District of Maharashtra State, India. It belongs to Vidarbh region . It belongs to Nagpur Division . It is located 99 KM towards South from District head quarters Gadchiroli. 6 KM from Aheri. 887 KM from State capital Mumbai Alapalli Pin code is 442703 and postal head office is Allapalli . Khamancheru ( 7 KM ) , Aheri ( 7 KM ) , Chinchagundi ( 9 KM ) , Mahagaon (bk.) ( 11 KM ) , Rajpur Patch ( 16 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Alapalli. Alapalli is surrounded by Mulchera Taluka towards North , Bejjur Taluka towards west , Etapalli Taluka towards East , Kouthala Taluka towards west .

FORESTS


Allapalli is very famous for the forest. The Allapalli forests are the source of the world-famous teak wood (tectona grandis). The 'Glory of Allapalli' which represents the surviving patch of the original forests before they were brought under scientific management more than a 100 years ago, is famous as a living museum of the forest ecology. It is located 7 km. from Allapalli on the Permili-Bhamaragadh road. 2 divisional offices of the Forest Department are placed in Allapalli.

Economy

Allapalli's revenue generate in different forms like it has a good market line, people are employees in many offices. Allapalli comes alive on Sunday, the traditional market day also known as Allapalli Sunday Market. People from surrounding villages and towns come to Allapalli to sell their wares. This fairs has a lot of local flavour and specialises in agricultural produce, vegetables, locally-grown fruit, spices, clothes and even plants. dental services are also available in alapalli
